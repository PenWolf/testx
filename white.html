SafeX will be running several projects in the crypto market. Projects will be launched over time as the volume of burning increases. We will not use time to define the projects launching schedule. Our projects will not be revealed quarterly.We believe that this approach will increase the value of the coin and motivate more people to hold it. We will be launching a project everytime 50 trillion “....” is burned. You can find a list of the projects in our roadmap to see where we are going and to stay on track. SafeX Coin is quite different from any other coin with a name that starts with safe because our coin is based on real projects that you will personally be able to experience and that you will find useful as a trader. We strongly believe that Blockchain technology and cryptocurrencies are the future. We are acutely aware that Blockchain technology and crypto will affect every industry and transform the shape of the future, that is why we will be implementing different projects in these areas. With safeX you will be part of the future.

SafeX Token

The token will help us lead the projects to true success. We believe that the value of the token will increase with the release of each and every project. It is for that reason that you will earn more with time as a coin holder.

Why won't we distribute the coin?

The free distribution of currency may result in Float Market which will lead to more inflation and drop the coin value.Our coin holders safety is in our top priorities. We want the token community and holders to become the token’s principal advocate...

Why should you hold?

Just like holding Binance Coin makes you a part of the coin project which is the largest crypto trading platform in the world, holding SafeX will make you a part of our projects… The coin value will increase with the launch and the completion of the projects listed in our roadmap which will prevent the whales from manipulating the currency valuations and Tokenomics.

Furthermore, we are planning to constantly shrink the supply by having monthly “burns” and to have constant buyback every two months to reduce the token circulating supply. These last points are particularly important when it comes to increasing the value of the token and trying to keep the community rewarded.

Plateformes listing

We believe in the power of our vision. We will be working to ensure that our token is listed in the top ranked exchange platforms and to have the traffic, liquidity, trading volumes that allows to be ranked in coinmarketcap.
